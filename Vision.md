## Atlas Platform v1 (PR-041)

**Atlas is an Evidence-Driven Enterprise Network Decision Platform** —
the first FounderOS product. It answers the questions network engineers
actually ask:

- *Why can't A reach B?* — Path Intelligence walks the evidence hop by
  hop and stops at the first deterministic failure.
- *What happens if I make this change?* — Prediction answers with blast
  radius, risk, and confidence before anything is touched.
- *What order should tonight's window run in?* — Compass plans many
  changes with evidence-based dependencies.
- *What changed overnight?* — Change intelligence compares every
  discovery against the last.
- *What is my enterprise, really?* — Federation builds one canonical
  graph from many observation points, with provenance for every merge.

**The one-network principle.** An enterprise has ONE network, observed
from multiple discovery points. Profiles are observation points —
credentials, seeds, schedules — never network boundaries. Every engine
reasons at enterprise scope by default; narrowing is a choice, never a
prerequisite.

**The evidence principle.** Atlas never guesses. Every conclusion cites
the evidence that produced it; confidence is documented arithmetic
capped below 100%; unknowns are stated, not papered over. Deterministic
reasoning comes before AI — a future assistant will *explain* Atlas's
conclusions, never *make* them.

**The workflow principle.** Engineers think in goals, not modules.
Mission — the landing page — asks "What are you trying to do?" and
orchestrates the engines. The GUI is the primary experience; every
capability is a service the CLI, REST clients, and the future assistant
reuse unchanged.
