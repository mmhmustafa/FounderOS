Audit

Every operator mutation Atlas has recorded, in one model: actor, timestamp, scope, operation, before/after, reason, source, and correlation id. Site overrides and identity resolutions are read from their own trails (their undo semantics stay exactly where they were); exceptions, assignments, acknowledgements, notes, and suppressions write here directly. Secret values never enter an audit event — references only.
